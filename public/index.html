how to use this

its simple! the page will show you a question. Think well if you know the answer to this (and don't lie! it won't help you).
If you do: press the "Yes" button.
If you don't: press the "No" button. We will give you some hints.
If you still don't: press the "No" button again. We will give you more specific hints.
If you still don't (poor child what happened to you lol): press the "No" button AGAIN. We will show you a possible answer.
